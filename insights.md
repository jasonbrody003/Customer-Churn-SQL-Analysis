# Customer Churn Analysis Insights

## Key Findings

1. The dataset contains **7043 telecom customers**.

2. **1869 customers churned**, while **5174 customers remained**, resulting in a churn rate of about **26.5%**.

3. Customers with **month-to-month contracts show the highest churn**, indicating that long-term contracts improve customer retention.

4. Customers who churn have **higher average monthly charges ($74.44)** compared to retained customers ($61.27).

5. **New customers (tenure < 12 months) churn the most**, suggesting early customer experience is critical for retention.
